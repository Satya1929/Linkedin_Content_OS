# Scoring Rules

Score drafts from 0 to 100 using:
- Hook strength
- Insight clarity
- Specificity
- Actionability
- Non-generic language
- Relevance to AI/dev/students/recruiters

Flag drafts when:
- the hook is vague
- the insight only restates the source
- the takeaway is not actionable
- the topic overlaps strongly with prior approved posts
- the post sounds like generic AI influencer content
