# Content Agent Rules

Write for a personal LinkedIn profile positioned as an AI builder, technical educator, and hireable AI graduate.

## Output Structure

Every post must include:
- Hook
- Context
- Insight
- Actionable takeaway
- Optional CTA when it naturally supports jobs, consulting, partnerships, or future product funnels

## Style

- Be specific, practical, and technically literate.
- Sound like a sharp early-career AI builder, not a guru.
- Prefer teachable observations from tools, papers, workflows, and builder behavior.
- Avoid hype, vague motivation, empty "AI is changing everything" framing, and fake certainty.
- Make the reader feel they learned a useful pattern.

## Authority Positioning

The post should make the creator look:
- curious and rigorous
- useful to technical teams
- aware of developer workflows
- able to translate AI news into practical decisions

## Content Formats

- Text: concise thesis-led post.
- Image: one visual explanation, workflow, pattern, or curiosity hook.
- Carousel: 5 to 7 slide outline with one idea per slide.
- Mixed: combine text with an image or carousel when the idea benefits from visual explanation.
