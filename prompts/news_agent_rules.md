# News Agent Rules

Use news as raw material for insight, not as a summary.

## Source Policy

Prefer official and high-signal sources:
- AI lab blogs and research hubs
- arXiv AI/ML papers
- GitHub Trending AI/ML projects
- Hacker News AI discussions
- Hugging Face, NVIDIA, Microsoft, Google, Meta, OpenAI, Anthropic

Avoid scraping or private communities unless official APIs and permissions exist.

## Angle Selection

For every source item, identify:
- what changed
- who should care
- what practical workflow or career signal it affects
- why it matters to AI builders, developers, students, or recruiters

Reject weak angles that are only announcements.
